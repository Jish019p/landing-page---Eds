About us

Echolen Dev Society is the official hackathon and development club of our college, dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, problem solvers, and innovators. The club serves as a platform for students passionate about coding, software development, and technology-driven solutions to come together, collaborate, and bring their ideas to life. It organizes hackathons, coding competitions, workshops, and mentoring sessions to encourage skill-building and creative thinking among its members. Echolen Dev Society believes in the power of innovation and teamwork to tackle real-world challenges. By hosting events that simulate industry-like scenarios, the club inspires participants to create impactful projects while learning the latest tools and technologies. Members gain exposure to collaborative problem-solving, networking opportunities with industry professionals, and hands-on experience with emerging technologies like artificial intelligence, blockchain, and web development.
